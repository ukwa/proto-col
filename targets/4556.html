Written by Knox Chitiyo, Associate Fellow, Africa Programme, Chatham House. Chitiyo writes about the influence of Mandela and the ANC on the character of liberation and black national movements throughout Africa during the 1960s and 1970s. This article also looks at Mandela's influence on South Africa's regional policies after 1994, in particular in the development of the Southern African Development Community (SADC).
