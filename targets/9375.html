This letter, dated 11 February 2014, addressed to the Chancellor of the Exchequer and written by Nicholas MacPherson, Permanent Secretary to H M Treasury, summarises the advice of the Treasury against currency union with an independent Scotland. The letter, and comments that it contained, was widely cited by the Scottish referendum 'No' campaign.
