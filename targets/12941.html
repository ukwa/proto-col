Orcadia & Other Stories is a major exhibition of work by Zoë Walker & Neil Bromwich at the Pier Arts Centre, Stromness, providing a survey of the artist’s work to date alongside new work created especially for the exhibition in Orkney. It is part of GENERATION a celebration of Scottish contemporary art of the last 25 years to mark the Commonwealth Games in Glasgow.
