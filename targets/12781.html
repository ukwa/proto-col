Starcatchers specialises in performances and creativity for babies, toddlers and young children under the age of 5 in Scotland.

Blue Block Studio is an exciting, innovative programme of creative play and storytelling for babies and young children aged 0-4 presented by Starcatchers, which visited 10 communities across Scotland as part of the Culture 2014 programme celebrating the Commonwealth Games.
