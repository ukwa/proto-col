July 2013 report from the think-tank IPPR, giving results and analysis of survey of national identity and attitudes in England. The report suggests that those more closely identifying as 'English' were also more likely to show dissatisfaction with both the constitutional structure of the UK (feeling that too great an advantage was offered to Scotland), and with England's role in Europe.
